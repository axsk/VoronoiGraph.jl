VoronoiGraph.boundary_area — Method

given two generators g1, g2, vertices of their common boundary (in generator representation) and the list of all generators, compute the boundary volume. It works by constructing a polytope from halfspaces between the generators of the boundary vertices. We use an affine transformation to get rid of the dimension in direction g1->g2

source
